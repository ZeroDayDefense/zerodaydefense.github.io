Services

Network Penetration Testing

Our network penetration testing services identify, validate, and prioritize vulnerabilities on internal, internet facing, and cloud-based IT infrastructure.

Web Application Testing

Zero Day Defense identifies security vulnerabilities in web applications that make your organization susceptible to external or internal security threats.

Red/Purple Teaming

We will help your organization identify and respond to real-world threats through breach scenarios, threat detection, and other attacks that simulates an adversary with malicious intent.

Social Engineering

We can provide your organization with a multitude of social engineering assessments. We can simulate a mass phishing email to your entire organization, a spear phishing designed for selected high risk employees, helpdesk ticket attacks, phone pretexting, and much more.

Mobile Application Testing

We can test your mobile application and identify vulnerabilities that put your organization at risk. We manually pentest for security controls in four essential areas: file system, memory, network communications, and graphical user interface. We test for the OWASP Top 10 and much more.

About

Zero Day Defense is a penetration testing company with focus on network, web application, mobile application penetration testing services, as well as social engineering assessments and red teaming.

Our meticulous and precise testing will ensure proper mitigation and guidance on how to resolve vulnerabilities that put your organization at risk. Whatever your security needs are, we strive to work with you to give you the best and most meaningful results.
